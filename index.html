2024 Board Highlights

2024 was a year marked by growth and a reaffirmation of core values at the Django Software Foundation. As board members, we remain dedicated to supporting, promoting, protecting, and advancing the Django Web Framework, with inclusivity and active community engagement at the forefront.

Our key milestones from 2024 include:

  • Getting Working Groups up and running: The creation and expansion of working groups continues to foster meaningful contributions from our global community. From Code of Conduct to Social Media, we‚Äôre elated to see where our volunteers take the Foundation and our community.
